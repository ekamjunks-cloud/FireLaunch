# Fire Launch - Product Requirements Document

## Original Problem Statement
Create a high-conversion website for a UK-based e-commerce launch studio called "Fire Launch".
- Positioning: Founder-led E-Commerce Launch Studio helping UK businesses launch structured Shopify and eBay systems in 14 days
- Tone: Strategic. Controlled. Confident. Powerful.

## User Personas
1. **UK Business Owners** - Looking to launch e-commerce stores on Shopify and eBay
2. **Established Brands** - Seeking multi-channel store setups with advanced features
3. **New Entrepreneurs** - Starting their first online store

## Core Requirements (Static)
- Light theme dominant with white background
- Fire-red primary buttons (#D92D20)
- Subtle ember glow on hover
- Clean SaaS grid design
- Sticky navigation
- Sticky "Book Free Call" button
- Mobile CTA bar
- No gradients, no flashy motion
- Calendly integration for booking

## What's Been Implemented (December 2025)

### Pages
1. **Homepage**
   - Hero section with animated red underline
   - Trust indicators (50+ stores, 14 days, £2M+ revenue, 100% UK)
   - Services section (5 cards in bento grid)
   - Pricing section (3 tiers with Growth highlighted)
   - Testimonials section (2 cards)
   - Final CTA section
   - Footer with links

2. **Demo Stores Page**
   - Gallery of 6 demo store previews
   - Store cards with images, descriptions, features
   - CTA buttons linking to Calendly

### Features
- Sticky navigation with Book Free Call button
- Mobile-responsive design with bottom CTA bar
- All buttons link to Calendly: https://calendly.com/firelaunch/30min
- Typography: Space Grotesk (headings) + Inter (body)
- Color system: Crimson Flame #D92D20, White, Cool Grey, Deep Slate, Charcoal Navy

### Pricing Tiers
- Starter Launch: £799 (25 products)
- Growth Launch: £999 - Most Popular (50 products)
- Authority Launch: £1,500 (100 products)
- Deposit model: 50% to begin, 50% before launch
